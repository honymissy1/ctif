WELCOME TO CTIF

(Christ Testimonials interdominational Fellowship)

Why God Established CTIF

"A gathering of Strong Kingdom Giant" the Lord has chosen us as youths to revive the youth of other denominations both in our territory and outside by the revelation of the word of God

VISION

  1. To study the deeper revelation of the power and the gospel of Christ
  2. To take the testimony of Christ to the nations of the world with undeniable evidences and tangible proof
  3. To help unite and prepare the youths in various denominations for the end time move of God
  4. To raise prayers for the restoration and revival in the body of Christ
MISSION

Discipleship

The core aspect of our ministry is discipleship, where we come together at the foot of the master(Jesus)

Seminars

Organizing seminars all across streets, cities, campuses, countries and all over the world

Conferences

Setting up conference both in locations we can reach and the ones we cannot physically

Crusades

Power crusades in all region to demonstrate the power and presence of God

Outreaches

Reaching out to the lost and restoring them back to Christ
